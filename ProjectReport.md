# LMS Project Report

## Project Summary
The LMS platform is a state-of-the-art educational ecosystem providing specialized dashboards for students, teachers, and admins to manage and engage with academic content effectively. It integrates advanced progress tracking via a focal-point study timer and automated PDF certification to validate student achievements. Innovative AI capabilities deliver personalized career roadmaps and real-time assistance through content summarization and interactive doubt-solving. This system transforms traditional learning into an engaging, data-driven experience with cinema-mode viewing and multi-difficulty assessment modules.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Multi-Role Authentication & Dashboards
Secure access control for Admins, Teachers, and Students. Each user receives a personalized dashboard featuring real-time statistics, recent activity, and quick-access navigation tailored to their role.

### 2. Comprehensive Course Management
Teachers can design structured learning paths using Chapters and Modules. The system supports various content formats, including PDFs, DOC files, Videos, Images, and external links, all served through Cloudinary integration.

### 3. Real-time Progress Tracking & Study Timer
A focal-point detection system tracks the time students spend on each lesson down to the second. It provides real-time feedback with ticking "Time Spent" and "Time Left" counters, ensuring minimum study requirements are met before unlocking quizzes.

### 4. Advanced Quiz System (Standard & Fast Track)
Modules feature integrated quizzes with weighted difficulty selection (Easy, Medium, Hard). "Fast Track" mode allows students to skip modules by passing a high-difficulty challenge, while "Standard" mode ensures comprehensive understanding.

### 5. AI-Powered Career Roadmap
Generates personalized study plans based on career goals, selected subjects, and current progress. It includes gap detection logic to automatically adjust learning paths if study targets are missed.

### 6. AI Learning Assistants
Integrated AI tools within the course viewer provide instant content summarization, practice question generation, and real-time doubt-solving to enhance student comprehension.

### 7. Automated Certification
The system automatically generates professional, verifiable PDF certificates of completion once a student has mastered 100% of a course's curriculum and met all study goal requirements.

### 8. Detailed Analytics & Grade Reports
Comprehensive tracking of student performance across all courses. Students can view their grade history, while teachers have access to detailed progress reports for all enrolled students.

### 9. Premium Course Viewer & Cinema Mode
A distraction-free "Cinema Mode" provides a premium viewing experience for lessons. The viewer includes integrated progress bars and easy navigation across modules.

### 10. Study Goal Notifications
A proactive notification system reminds students of their daily and weekend study targets, providing motivational feedback and progress updates to keep learning on track.
